===============LORE===============

In the age before ages, when the worlds were still raw and unshaped, there existed a single point of impossible brilliance — a pulse of creation suspended between light and void. The ancients called it the Astral Nexus , the heart of every realm, and the axis upon which all magic turned. It was not forged, nor discovered; it simply was, older than the Overworld, deeper than the Nether, and brighter than the End’s eternal sky. From the Nexus flowed the laws of reality: gravity, motion, matter, silence, endurance, sight, destruction, and preservation. Eight forces, perfectly balanced, perfectly bound. But alance is fragile… and power invites hunger. When the Nexus began to surge beyond control, threatening to tear the realms into drifting shards, the Three Wardens made the ultimate choice — a choice that would echo through eternity. They shattered it ones and for good . . .

The explosion of astral light rippled across every dimension, carving rifts in the sky and shaking the bedrock of creation. When the brilliance faded, the Nexus was gone… replaced by eight artifacts, each infused with a fragment of its cosmic essence. Armor, weapons, tools — mundane shapes hiding impossible power, they are known as The 8 Echoes/Artifacts of Nexus. Fearing what might happen if these Echoes were ever reunited, the Wardens scattered them across the world. One was buried beneath a mountain that floats in reverse gravity. Another sealed in a labyrinth that rearranges itself with every breath. Others lost in collapsing timelines, forgotten temples, or drifting islands between realms. No map marks their resting places. No mortal remembers their names. Only the Three Wardens know the truth: the Nexus is not dead. It sleeps — dreaming through its fragments, waiting for the day when the Echoes will awake and call one another once more. And now, after millennia of silence… the world trembles again. Strange lights flicker in abandoned ruins. Stars shift in patterns unseen since the First Age. Whispers rise from the deepest places of the DeadVerse Planet. The Astral Nexus is stirring. And someone — somewhere — is destined to find its Echoes.
